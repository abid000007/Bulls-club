About us

BULLS F.C. is revolutionizing football in Pakistan, setting new standards of excellence and passion in the sport. Founded by AFC and FA England certified Coach Mudasar Mukhtar, our academy is dedicated to developing the next generation of football talent. With over 500 players currently enrolled across 6 strategic locations in Lahore and Multan, BULLS F.C. has become a prominent name in the local football scene. Since our inception, we've trained and shaped over 5,000 players, providing them with top-tier coaching, a supportive environment, and opportunities to grow both as athletes and individuals. Our approach focuses on skill development, teamwork, and fostering a love for the game, creating not just players, but leaders on and off the field. At BULLS F.C., we are more than just a football academy—we are a family, united by our love for the sport and our commitment to success. WE ARE BULLS. WE ARE A TEAM. Whether you're a beginner looking to get started or an experienced player aiming to take your skills to the next level, BULLS F.C. is the place for you. Join us and be part of something bigger—an academy that pushes boundaries, builds character, and plays the game with heart.
